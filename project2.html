This uses the formula:

\[\frac{V^2}{X} = Q\]

Where V is voltage in volts, X is reactance in ohms and Q is reactive power in var.

For capacitors we have \(X = \frac{1}{2 \pi f C}\), and for inductors we have \(X = 2 \pi f L\), where \(f\) is the frequency in hertz, \(C\) is the capacitance in farads and \(L\) is the inductance in henrys.
